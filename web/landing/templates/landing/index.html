Services

Communication Skills

Our communication skills program helps participants develop the skills they need to communicate clearly, confidently, and effectively with others. Through lectures, exercises, and role-plays, participants learn how to adapt their communication style to different audiences, give and receive feedback, and resolve conflicts.

Critical Thinking

Our critical thinking program helps individuals and organizations analyze and evaluate information objectively, without letting personal biases or emotions get in the way. Through lectures, and exercises participants learn how to identify and overcome cognitive biases, ask probing questions, and make more informed decisions.

Listening Skills

Our listening skills program helps participants listen actively, retain information, and communicate effectively with others. Through interactive activities and role-plays, participants learn how to listen with empathy, ask clarifying questions, and give constructive feedback.

Care For A Little story

Here's a little backdrop as to how soft work was inspired. So in the beginning, there were two basic problems:
  1. 1. Professionals who had a hard time processing their thoughts for problem solving (especially through digital means)
  2. 2. Professionals who had developed a complex thought process that only machines could now understand them (funny right? but true)
And that's where "Soft Work" comes in. "Soft" indicative of soft skills because we exist to help professionals develop clear cut mental pathways to write definitive codes to machines and also to create a balance that helps technical fellows articulate these complex ideas to their teams for seamless collaboration.
